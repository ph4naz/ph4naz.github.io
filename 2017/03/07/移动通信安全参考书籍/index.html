以下为Valtteri Niemi老师出版的一些书，想要选课的同学可以提前参考下。课本具体使用哪本书以及先修课程暂时还不清楚，还需要等后续的通知。(`・ω・´)

  2. V. Niemi and Kaisa Nyberg, “UMTS security”, John Wiley & Sons, Chichester, 2003, 273+12p.
  3. Silke Holtmanns, V. Niemi, Philip Ginzboorg, Pekka Laitinen and N.Asokan: “Cellular Authentication
    for Mobile and Internet Services”, John Wiley & Sons, Chichester, 2008, 204+10 p.
  4. Dan Forsberg, Günther Horn, Wolf-Dietrich Moeller and V. Niemi: “LTE security”, John Wiley & Sons, Chichester, 2010, 284+13 p.
